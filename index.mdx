## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up at [app.stackmap.io](https://app.stackmap.io) and log in to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first project">
    Enter your app's domain, upload a `.env` file, or add services manually. StackMap will scan and discover your infrastructure automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your graph">
    Your infrastructure appears as an interactive node graph. Click any service card to see details, status, and upcoming expiry dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your `.env` file is processed entirely in your browser — secret values are stripped before anything leaves your device. Only key names and detected provider types are stored.
</Tip>
